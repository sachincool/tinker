TP splits each matmul across 8 GPUs: all-reduce every layer, so it stays on NVLink inside the node.
PP splits layers into stages across nodes: cheap point-to-point, crosses the network fine.
DP / FSDP replicates the whole thing and all-reduces gradients on top.
