Diagnosis · serving metric + GPU metric
Why is the server slow?
The serving metric says something is wrong. The GPU metric next to it says which kind of wrong.
read the queue, the KV cache, and the DCGM pipes together
one panel alone lies; the pair localizes the bottleneck
▽
queue-bound
serving: num_requests_waiting high, TTFT high
GPU: SM_ACTIVE / GPU-util actually low
→ not a GPU problem. Add replicas (part 5). Don't buy a bigger card.
memory-bound · decode
serving: ITL degrading under load
GPU: DRAM_ACTIVE pinned, TENSOR_ACTIVE low
→ smaller batch, quantize, watch for preemption thrash.
compute-bound · prefill
serving: TTFT high on long prompts
GPU: TENSOR_ACTIVE saturated, DRAM lower
→ chunked prefill, cap prompt length, or disaggregate prefill.
